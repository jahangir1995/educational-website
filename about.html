Comparison and opportunity

After completing SSC level (10th grade, also know as 'O' level, SSC vocational or Dhakhil' level) a student can pursue his or her educational career for obtaining a "Diploma in Engineering" diploma degree which falls in "Technical Education System" from polytechnic Institute, or they can obtain HSC (12th grade, also known as 'A' level, or "Intermediate" level, or 'Alim' level) certificate degree, which falls in "General Education System", From various other academic collage, and HSC being the mainstream study line in south Asia Countries. HSC courses focus mainly on various Science , Arts, Commerce, Business (Applied & Parctical) areas

The "Diplome-in-Engineering" diploma degree level is rated higher than the HSC certificate degree level, and both are lesser than a formal Baccalaurrate or BSc (Bachelor in Science) degree, and both are also the entry level for enrolling into any B.Sc Engineering degree. Students when complete the Diploma-in-Engineering (4 years curriculum) courses, they acquire educational level equivalent to grade XlV (14th grade),[1]and students who complete HSC level(exactly 2 yearslong curriculum), they acquire educational level equivalent to grade X11 (12th grade) . Most of the diploma-in-Engineering curriculum were extended from a 3 years study period to a 4 years long study period. In "Madrashah Education System", the 14th grade is also known as "Fazil" level.

"Diploma-in-Engineering", a diploma degree should not to be confused whit a European, America "Diploma", which usually has only 1 year or less long educational curriculum for obtaining a (Diploma)"certificate", a Degree. In those geographical areas, various Science & other Academic & Technical field subjects are offered for obtaining an "Associate" degree which includes a minimum of 2 years long curriculum, and also transferable toward obtaining a Bachelor degree, comes closest to a South-Asia "Diploma-in-Engineering" degree, where such "Associate" degree is still 2 years less than a South-Asia degree.Enven thouth there are few other type of "Associate" degree (1 year 2 year) are offered in some Occupational of Vocational or various trade Training areas, but since Those are (usually) nontransferable,[2] so such "Associate" degree are also much lesser than and not equivalent to a South-Asia "Diploma-in-Engineering" degree.
